About Me

Ngoc Nguyen is a Full Stack Developer. He's enjoyed coding and designing websites since 2000. He graduated with a Bachelor degree in Computer Science at California State University of Northridge in 2004.

He worked as Dialer Administrator at Chase Bank call center from 2004 - 2006. He worked as Oracle Administrator/Developer at Munchkin Inc. from 2006 - 2011. He is a CEO/Founder of PN TRADING INC. from 2011 - Present. He's been a web administrator for NorthHollywoodChurch.org since 2004. He enjoys traveling, cooking, gardening, and of course coding.
